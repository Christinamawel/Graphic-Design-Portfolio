About Me

After losing my job due to COVID, I began studying to further my career in the tech field. I started studying on my own, and within a few months, I had become proficient in Photoshop, Illustrator, and Adobe XD, as well as coding languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and ReactJS. I then began studying at Epicodus coding school, where I deepened my knowledge of coding languages and UI design while creating over 80 projects remotely with a team. Through my studies, I found a passion for creating beautiful, eye-catching content that is also user-friendly. I love the challenge of balancing beauty with intuitive design and feel that no design should come at the expense of the message the media is trying to convey.

After graduating from the course, I became a teacher at Epicodus, helping over 40 students to learn to code and design applications of their own. This helped me build even greater communication skills and an understanding of how to guide. I feel now that teaching is much like designing any piece of media, as both require you to guide your audience to an understanding of a message.

I then worked as a Graphic Designer for FastSigns, where I designed signage for hundreds of clients. I loved this work, even the seemingly tedious warning signs or street signs, as they all came with their unique messages to convey and information to organize in a cohesive way. I loved dealing with the small details of a sign and seeing them build into a picture that could be taken in as quickly as possible. I am learning and growing as a designer and hope to continue developing my skills while creating beautiful media that will always convey the intended message to every user.
